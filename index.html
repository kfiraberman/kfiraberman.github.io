Short Bio


I am currently a researcher in the Reality Capture Group at the AICFVE, Beijing. In parallel, I am pursuing my Ph.D in Tel-Aviv University, under the supervision of Prof. Daniel Cohen-Or. My areas of intersts include deep neural networks architectures for computer graphics application. My B.Sc. (summa cum laude) and M.Sc. (cum laude) in Electrical Engineering from the Technion were done under the supervision of Prof. Yonina C. Eldar. I have extensive experience in code writing and algorithms development, particularly in the field of Visual Artificial Intelligence (AI), as a 4 year algorithm designer and developer and a 2 year team leader of the algorithms group in the satellites branch of the IDI.
